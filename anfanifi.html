Overview

The Customer

AnfaniFI is a platform that connects businesses and individuals across the African supply chain. It empowers businesses to link opportunities with finance. By assessing operational, financial, and behavioral data, AnfaniFI creates a network of trusted partners.

AnfaniFI launched Anfani360 in Sierra Leone. Anfani360 is a restaurant solution designed to empower Africa's food service businesses. It offers a variety of features to help restaurants grow their business, including online ordering, a 360 view of operations management, and driver management.

Project Goals

AnfaniFi's flagship product, Anfani360, was initially built on PHP. Recognizing the need for modernization, the project aimed to transition the codebase to a more current framework, enhancing performance, scalability, and maintainability. The goal was to soft launch in Sierra Leone, focusing on acquiring early users, gathering their invaluable feedback, and creating a data-driven roadmap for future development.

The Approach

Research

During my time in Sierra Leone, I conducted immersive, on-the-ground research with key stakeholders across the food delivery ecosystem — from consumers and restaurants to delivery companies. Through interviews, observations, and analysis, I uncovered pain points and opportunities for digitization, which informed the development of AnfaniFi's solutions tailored to the unique African context.

Ideation

I mapped out existing workflows, analyzed pain points, and brainstormed potential improvements alongside key stakeholders. Through collaborative workshops, we refined processes and envisioned a future state where AnfaniFi played a central role in optimizing operations. Crucially, I ensured that all proposed solutions were tightly aligned with our North Star metric, guaranteeing strategic focus and impact

Requirements Gathering & Prioritization

I spearheaded the requirements gathering process, prioritizing features based on user needs and business goals. I then developed a high-level product roadmap, outlining key milestones and release timelines. Utilizing user story mapping, I collaborated with the development team to break down requirements into epics and user stories, ensuring a clear understanding of scope for each sprint. We aimed for a two-week sprint cadence to maintain agility and responsiveness to feedback.

The Solution

Menu Creation

I designed and implemented an intuitive menu creation experience, including a novel "selection group" feature for easy customization. This streamlined the process for restaurant owners, saving time and reducing complexity. The menu creation process was designed to accommodate almost any variation of meal, offering unparalleled flexibility to the user.
